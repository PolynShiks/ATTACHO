FAQs
What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?
Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend skills with projects in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building.
Is Frontend Mentor free?
Yes, Frontend Mentor offers both free and premium challenges to help you improve your coding skills.
Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?
Absolutely! Completing projects from Frontend Mentor is a great way to showcase your skills in your portfolio.
How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge?
You can join the Frontend Mentor community on Discord or check the discussion threads for guidance and support.
